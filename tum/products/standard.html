All this in a pill?!

This supplement contains 4 high quality strains of gut-friendly microbes. All survive transit through the stomach. When they reach the intestinal system the spores germinate and the bacterial proliferate and work synergistically with other friendly gut microbes to combat pathogens, support a healthy microbiome throughout the intestinal system, and support a healthy, functioning intestinal barrier. These bacteria are not new to the human gut they are normally present but they are often in inadequate numbers to provide the high level of g.i. support needed when traveling to exotic destinations. The high-quality probiotic supplement is also very travel friendly and does not require refrigeration.

Each of these species has scientific research demonstrating both safety and efficacy in humans, both children and adult. 3 This is a food supplement and it is not intended to prevent, treat, nor diagnose any medical conditions. Based on results of recent research it has been recommended that probiotics may not be appropriate for severely ill or immune-compromised individuals due to a risk of fungicemia and bacteriocemia.
